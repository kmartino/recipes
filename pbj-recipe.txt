Ingredients:
 - 2 pieces of bread
 - tablespoon of peanut butter
 - tablespoon of jelly

Directions:
 1. On one side of one piece of bread spread peanut butter

 2. On one side of second piece of bread spread jelly.

 3. Bring two pieces of bread together, the jelly and peanut butter sides facing one another.

Enjoy!
